Guidance: Prof. Bhaskar Bhatt, IIT Gandhinagar; Prof. Ken Pickar, Caltech

Designing product for construction labor Carrying heavy loads on head, these construction laborers walk several mile everyday and carry materials up-down across building storeys using barely made stairways. The project involved Interviewing the construction workers to find their pressing health needs, and developing and prototyping a product to cater to the specific requirements through design methodology concepts.



The most pressing needs were carrying loads vertically and excessive use of arm strength
Our solution was a paddle operated portable crane that could transfer loads across two storeys. Further step would be to electrically operate the crane.
